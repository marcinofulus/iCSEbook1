.. admonition:: Definicja silni.
    
.. math::
     
     \left\{
     \begin{array}{ll}
     0!=1  & {} \\ 
     n!=n \cdot (n-1)!, & {} n>0 \\
     \end{array}
     \right.

Dwumian Newtona

.. math::

   \ {n \choose k} = \frac {n!}{k! \cdot (n-k)!}, ~ n \ge k \ge 0  

.. sagecellserver::

   n=5
   k=2
   print binomial(n,k)

   a=int(7*random())

Wynik
